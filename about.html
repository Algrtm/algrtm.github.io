What this is

This project began as a merger of my hobbies: programming, photography and digital art. The result - high resolution and quality abstract art pieces based on algorithms and math equations.

Who I am

How I create

I wrote custom software that allows me to enter any algorithm and/or math equation, tune parameters and render art in extremely (or unlimited, given enough RAM) high resolutions.
